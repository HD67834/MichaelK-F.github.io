Hover 1


With it's simple but futureistic design, The Hover 1 is the ideal hovercar for anyone. Starting from $15,000(CAD), the Hover 1 can drive about 700 kilometers(434 miles) on one charge, and only takes 5 minutes to fully charge the battery. The Hover 1 can drive autonomusly, so if you want to go to work and there are no parking spots, you can leave the car and it will find a parking spot for you, and when you need it again you can summon the car using the provided app.
    Features:
  • Can drive 700 kilometers(434 miles) on one charge
  • Takes 5 minutes to fully charge the battery
  • Has fully autonomus driving
  • Has self tinting glass(feature can be turned off)
  • Also has voice activation
